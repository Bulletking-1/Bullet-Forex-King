Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

Smart Money Concepts is about understanding how the largest market participants — banks, hedge funds, and institutions — trade the markets. By learning SMC strategies, you can spot liquidity zones, fair value gaps, and order blocks that drive price action.

Liquidity

Liquidity grabs occur when price manipulates above or below key highs and lows to collect orders before reversing. Recognizing liquidity levels is essential to avoid false breakouts and align with smart money moves.

Order Flow

Order flow shows how institutions place large trades. By analyzing order blocks and imbalances, traders can anticipate where price will move next. This gives you a roadmap to follow the smart money.

Premium & Discount Zones

Price often trades between premium (sell zones) and discount (buy zones). Identifying these areas allows traders to enter trades at optimal levels.
