Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

Smart Money Concepts (SMC) focuses on how major financial institutions move the markets. Unlike retail strategies that rely on lagging indicators, SMC is based on price action, liquidity engineering, and order flow.

  • Order Blocks: Zones where institutions place large buy or sell orders.
  • Fair Value Gaps: Imbalances in price that tend to be filled before continuation.
  • Market Structure: Identifying the true trend using swing highs and lows.

Liquidity

Liquidity is the lifeblood of forex trading. Institutions manipulate liquidity to enter and exit large positions without causing massive price shocks. Understanding where liquidity lies is crucial.

  • Stop hunts occur around previous highs and lows.
  • Liquidity zones often act as reversal points.
  • Avoid trading in the middle of a range; wait for liquidity sweeps.

Order Flow

Order flow analysis allows you to align with institutional moves rather than fighting them. It involves observing how orders are filled and where imbalance remains in the market.

  • Watch for mitigation blocks — areas where price returns to fill unexecuted orders.
  • Track market sentiment shifts to anticipate continuation or reversal.
  • Combine with premium/discount zones for optimal entry.

Premium & Discount Zones

Price operates within a constant tug-of-war between premium (selling) and discount (buying) zones. Entering in the right zone maximizes your risk-to-reward ratio.

  • Premium Zone: Look for selling opportunities above equilibrium.
  • Discount Zone: Look for buying opportunities below equilibrium.
  • Confluence with liquidity grabs = high-probability trades.
